Hello!

I am a writer, scholar, and website developer currently based in Brooklyn, New York. I am currently working on an academic paper entitled Aesthetics of Queer Asian American Motherhoods, a poetry collection centered on teenage solitude, and several zines dedicated to urban artistry.

On this page, you will find a portfolio of my work, ranging from creative writing to website development. My focus is largely on portfolio websites such as this one and editorial projects, but I am always eager to take on new challenges!

Feel free to take a look around and reach out with questions and inquiries! I’m always looking to take on new projects, grow my creative community, and support other artists!
